A little bit about us:

Who We Are:

We are a dynamic collective, founded in 2020 and reinstated in 2021 with a
passion for creating top-notch products. Our team includes five talented members,
each bringing unique expertise to the table. We began as an international gaming clan
but now we're inventing innovative applications that reflect our vision for the future.



Our Goal: Making Quality Tools Accessible to Everyone:

We have an array of objectives, but our primary aim is to serve and support our community.
In 2022, we made a commitment to offer all of our software for free and open-source usage
which is an impressive feat that we are proud of. Our aspiration is to ensure equitable access
to quality tools for everyone across the globe, regardless of who it may be or circumstance.



The Future: Collaborating and Progressing Together:

We're excited about the future and the opportunities it holds. We see ourselves growing and
evolving alongside our community, working towards mutual goals. Space exploration is a dream
that we're eager to pursue, and we'll continue to develop innovative products that push the
boundaries of what's possible.Our focus remains on creating enjoyable experiences that keep
our users coming back for more. So why not join us on this journey of progress and innovation!
