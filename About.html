Who are we?

We are a group that was originally founded in 2020 but reinstated in 2021.
We currently consist of 7 members each with their respective roles,
We originated as an international clan in numerous games,
and now here we are, creating applications and setting out the future!

What is our goal?

Our goals, we have many, however our primary goal is to help our community.
In 2022 we vowed to make ALL of our software free AND open source, amazing, we know.
We want to make sure everyone can access the same quality tools and so, here we are!

So... what about the future?

Where do we see ourselves in 1, 5, 10 years? Alive hopefully.
Jokes aside, we want to keep being close with out community and help eachother grow.
We also have our own ambitions, if we get the opportunity, we'd love to engage in space exploration.
We want to continue developing only the top notch products for the best enjoyment out of them possible.
And to grow together as a whole, because we always want to keep evolving and developing, together, ofcourse!
